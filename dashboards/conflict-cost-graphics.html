Editorial correction · Appended graphics
Graphics 2 & 3 above reflect the Oct–Nov 2025 government shutdown, not Operation Epic Fury
The Friday delay line and airline losses data are real and accurate — but they belong to a separate story: the 43-day FAA staffing crisis caused by the Oct 1, 2025 federal shutdown. They have no connection to Epic Fury. The graphics below correct the record with the actual aviation impact of the Feb 28, 2026 U.S.–Israel strikes on Iran: a sudden, international airspace collapse of a different order entirely.
